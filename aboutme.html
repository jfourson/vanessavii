MY STORY

Vanessa Vii is an international New York-based model known for her striking presence and versatile look. Her work spans over 4+ years of professional modeling experience across editorial, runway, beauty, and commercial work, including international campaigns for Shiseido, L’Oréal, KAO, MAC Cosmetics.


She believes modeling is not just about aesthetics, it's about telling a story through movement, light, emotion, fashion and perspective.


Beyond the camera, Vanessa is passionate about creating art, traveling, and creating online content on social platforms that varies across fashion, lifestyle, and comedy, while maintaining her most authentic self to share with her audencies.
