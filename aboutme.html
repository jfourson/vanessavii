VANESSA VII

MY STORY

Vanessa Vii is a New York–based fashion model known for her striking presence and versatile look. Originally from Ukraine, Vanessa’s journey into modeling began with a love for visual art and self-expression. Her work spans runway, editorial, advertising, and high-fashion campaigns, bringing authenticity and emotion to every shoot.


Beyond the camera, Vanessa is passionate about travel, sustainability, and the evolving intersection of fashion and culture. She believes modeling is not just about aesthetics — it's about telling a story through movement, light, and perspective. Her creative energy and professionalism have earned her collaborations with both emerging and global brands.


When she’s not on set, Vanessa enjoys exploring new cities, curating digital art, and connecting with her audience through her growing social platforms.
